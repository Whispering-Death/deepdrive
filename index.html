About

Vasuki Seshadri graduated in engineering from the Birla Institute of Technology Mesra and subsequently completed his Masters in Business Administration from the Moore School of Business at the University of South Carolina. He has published books - “Happiness and the Art of Being”, “The Practice of Self Enquiry” ,“Practising Who Am I” and “Life Beyond Death”.

Vasuki‘s unique background and an approachable demeanour enables him to mentor people from diverse age groups and various walks of life. He is extremely passionate about getting people to be present and raising the bar all the time. DeepDrive echoes his strong belief that holistic growth is the need of the hour. His mentorship aims to work at all levels of the individual resulting in both personal and professional transformation.

At the core of DeepDrive is the belief that anybody can change and transform themselves to be truly 'alive' !
