About Me

With over 20 years corporate experience, Vasuki Seshadri graduated in engineering from the Birla Institute of Technology Mesra and subsequently completed his Masters in Business Administration from Moore School of Business at the University of South Carolina. A self-taught scratch golfer, Vasuki has been coaching golf for over eight years. His personal inner awakening coincided with his golf journey and post August 2002 life was just not the same. He has published books, including, “Happiness and the Art of Being”, “The Practice of Self Enquiry” and “Practising Who Am I”. He has also edited and published a book titled “Life Beyond Death” in 2012. Vasuki‘s unique background and an approachable demeanour enables him to mentor people from diverse age groups (16-70 years) and walks of life. He is extremely passionate about peak performance in any sphere of life and DeepDrive echoes his strong belief that holistic growth is the need of the hour. His mentorship aims to work at all levels of the individual resulting in both personal and professional transformation.
